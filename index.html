Why HerbAl?

Preserving Traditional Herbal Knowledge

Knowledge about medicinal plants is often passed through generations, but it is now becoming less accessible to younger users. Many people rely on unreliable online sources, which may lead to incorrect plant identification and improper usage.

HerbAl addresses this gap by combining artificial intelligence with structured herbal information to create a more reliable, localized, and accessible learning tool.

Our Solution

AI-Powered Herbal Identification

HerbAl uses a MobileNetV2-based model to analyze leaf images and identify medicinal plants. It then presents useful information such as description, scientific name, and planting guide to help users better understand each plant.

The application also supports offline usage for scanning, searching, and viewing herbal information. Only the sign-in feature requires an internet connection.
